Glass Onion

Co-founder of a tech company, Miles Bron, invites his group of closest friends to his private island for a murder mystery party. He is surprised by the arrival of Benoit Blanc, the private detective, and his former partner and the other co-founder of their company, Andi. With a group of people who all have gained reasons to resent Miles, the murder mystery party soon turns to an actual murder investigation.

Wake Up Dead Man

A Catholic priest, Jud Duplenticy, is sent to a small-town church to be an assistant priest due to punching a deacon at his former parish. Jud quickly discovers that this church is extremely corrupt, mainly at the hands of the leader, Monsignor Jefferson Wicks. The two priests make their distaste for one another very public, so when Wicks is found during service with a knife in his back, Jud becomes suspect number one. A private detective is hired, Benoit Blanc, and Jud sees a way he could get out of this situation. He agrees to help Blanc, and finds himself going deeper and deeper into the history of the town and its church.
