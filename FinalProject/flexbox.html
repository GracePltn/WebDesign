Acrylic

Dries very quickly. Can be used on canvas, paper, wood, and more. Does not require special tools and is not expensive.

Oil

Dries slowly, but has a deep and rich color.

Watercolor

Transparent appearance, depending on how much water is mixed witht he paint.

Tempera

Centuries old. Fast-drying and results in a matte finish. Can easily crack.

Digital

Digital devices such as an iPad, computer, or graphics tablet. Many software programs exist to get started.
